```css
✅ Taupe: #C9B8A8
```

### ✅ Typography

```css
✅ Headings: Cormorant Garamond (serif)
✅ Body: Inter (sans-serif)
✅ Monospace: JetBrains Mono
✅ Proper hierarchy (H1: 48-64px, H2: 36-48px, etc.)
```

### ✅ Design Principles

1. ✅ **Minimalism First**
   - Generous white space
   - Clean layouts
   - No clutter

2. ✅ **Natural Materials Feel**
   - Earthy tones
   - Organic shapes (8-12px border radius)
   - Natural imagery

3. ✅ **Typography Hierarchy**
   - Clear heading sizes
   - Readable body text (16-18px)
   - Proper line-height (1.6)

4. ✅ **Spacing System**
   - Consistent spacing (4px, 8px, 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px, 64px, 96px)

5. ✅ **Components Style**
   - Buttons: Primary (brown), Secondary (outline), Ghost
   - Cards: White bg, beige border, subtle shadow
   - Images: 3:4 aspect ratio, rounded corners
